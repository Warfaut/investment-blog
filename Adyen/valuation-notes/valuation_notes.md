# 1. Valuation Methodologies

Adyen will be valued using three standard approaches:

## **1) Relative Valuation (Multiples)**  
Based on comparable companies in the payments & fintech industry.

Key metrics to be used:
- **EV / Sales**
- **EV / EBITDA**
- **EV / EBIT**
- **P / E**
- **Price / Free Cash Flow**

---

## **2) Intrinsic Valuation (DCF)**  
Discounting future free cash flows using:

- WACC  
- Revenue growth assumptions  
- Margin expansion expectations  
- Terminal value (Gordon Growth or Exit Multiple)

---

## **3) Cross-Check (Rule of 40 & FCF Yield)**  
Useful for high-growth payment processors.
